# Voice Cast and Direction

Trench Runner 2086 uses six pre-rendered English voice performances. They are generated locally with the open-weight Kokoro neural speech model, then edited and mastered as fixed offline WAV assets.

## Cast

- CONTROL: `af_heart`, calm mission command
- NEXUS INTERCEPT: `af_bella`, cold automated defense network
- ARCHON: `am_fenrir`, lowered and spatially processed antagonist

## Featured Lines

- "Flight link confirmed. Vanguard, you are cleared to engage."
- "Training complete. Weapons free."
- "Unauthorized craft detected. Core defenses active."
- "You crossed a graveyard to reach me. Now join it."
- "Core collapse confirmed. Full boost. Get out now."
- "We hear you, pilot."

Runtime browser speech synthesis is not used. Subtitles remain enabled by default, and secondary radio messages stay text-only so the featured performances retain impact.
