Human direction // Codex execution

Built by iteration,
not by accident.

The project began as a rough cockpit prototype. Direct play feedback shaped every major decision: aiming was rebuilt around the fixed reticle, weapons became true dual cannons, weak sound was replaced with layered procedural audio, unsuccessful gate mechanics were removed, and dense second-wave slowdowns were profiled and fixed.

Codex did more than generate code. It diagnosed regressions, preserved working systems, automated benchmarks, produced deterministic press captures and rebuilt the trailer as 1,800 frame-accurate images.
